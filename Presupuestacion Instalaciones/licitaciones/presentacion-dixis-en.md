## About Us

Dixis Recursos y Soluciones S.L. is a Menorca-based company specialized in technical installations, systems integration and IoT solutions for critical infrastructure. We are part of a **group of branch offices** with presence across the Balearic Islands, allowing us to provide local support with the backing of a broader organizational structure.

We develop projects in highly demanding environments, including the airport sector and public administrations in the Balearic Islands. Our secure project credentials enable us to work on classified and restricted-access facilities.
